## Introduction

Learning in public is about sharing your process, not just your results. This guide covers:

- Why learning in public works
- How to get started
- Common fears and how to overcome them

## Why It Works

- Attracts feedback and opportunities
- Builds a network
- Documents your progress

## Getting Started

- Share notes, not just polished work
- Use social media or a blog
- Be honest about what you’re learning

## Overcoming Fears

- Everyone starts somewhere
- Mistakes are part of the process
- Focus on progress, not perfection

## Conclusion

Learning in public is a growth multiplier. Start today.
