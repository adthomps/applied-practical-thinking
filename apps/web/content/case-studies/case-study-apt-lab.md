## Background

APT Lab was tasked with building a new product under tight deadlines and ambiguous requirements.

## Approach

- Used systems mapping to clarify goals
- Rapid prototyping to test ideas quickly
- Continuous feedback from stakeholders

## Results

- Delivered MVP in 6 weeks
- Reduced delivery time by 40%
- Improved team satisfaction and engagement

## Lessons Learned

- Systems thinking helps align teams
- Prototyping reduces risk
- Early feedback is critical
