## Introduction

Speed matters, but learning matters more. This post shares lessons from projects where rapid prototyping and continuous delivery led to better outcomes.

## Why Ship Fast?

- Get feedback sooner
- Reduce risk of building the wrong thing
- Build momentum

## How to Ship Fast

- Use prototypes to test ideas
- Automate deployment
- Keep scope small

## Learning Loops

- Review what worked and what didn’t
- Share lessons with the team
- Adjust process based on feedback

## Conclusion

Shipping fast is a means to an end: learning what works. Make learning the goal, not just speed.
