## Introduction

Feedback isn’t just a way to fix mistakes—it’s a multiplier for team effectiveness. Here’s why.

## Why Feedback Matters

- Surfaces issues early
- Builds trust
- Accelerates learning

## How to Build Feedback Loops

- Schedule regular feedback sessions
- Make feedback specific and actionable
- Close the loop: follow up on changes

## Conclusion

Teams that embrace feedback move faster and build better products.
