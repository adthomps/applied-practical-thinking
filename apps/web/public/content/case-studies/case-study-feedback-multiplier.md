## Background

The team was missing deadlines and morale was low.

## Approach

- Introduced regular feedback sessions
- Modeled open communication
- Celebrated small wins

## Results

- Doubled team velocity
- Improved retention
- Stronger team culture

## Lessons Learned

- Feedback is a habit
- Celebrate progress
- Culture change takes time
