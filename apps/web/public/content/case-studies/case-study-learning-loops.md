## Background

The team struggled with slow feedback and unclear goals.

## Approach

- Introduced regular feedback sessions
- Used metrics to track progress
- Encouraged open communication

## Results

- Higher product quality
- Faster iteration cycles
- Better team morale

## Lessons Learned

- Feedback is a multiplier
- Metrics drive focus
- Culture matters
