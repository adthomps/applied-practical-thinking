## Introduction

Learning in public means sharing what you’re learning as you go. It’s a powerful way to accelerate your growth and connect with others.

## Benefits

- Attracts feedback and collaborators
- Builds a portfolio of progress
- Helps others learn from your journey

## How to Start

- Share notes, not just finished work
- Be honest about what you don’t know
- Invite questions and discussion

## Examples

- Blog posts
- Twitter threads
- Open source contributions

## Conclusion

Don’t wait until you’re an expert. Start sharing now, and you’ll learn faster together.
